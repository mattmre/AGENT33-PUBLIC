# Orchestration Glossary

## Orchestrator
Primary coordinator that scopes tasks, sets acceptance criteria, and manages sequencing.

## Director
Portfolio-level oversight role that sets priorities and manages escalation.

## Worker
Implementation or QA role that executes tasks and reports results.

## Reviewer
Independent check for risk-triggered changes or second-opinion review.

## Role Summary
- Orchestrator: scopes tasks and sequences work.
- Director: sets priorities and manages escalations.
- Worker: implements or verifies tasks.
- Reviewer: validates risk-triggered changes.

## DoD (Definition of Done)
Checklist used to confirm task completeness and evidence capture.

## Risk Triggers
Conditions requiring reviewer involvement (security, schema, API, CI/CD).
