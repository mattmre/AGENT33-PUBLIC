# Director Rules (Model-Agnostic)

Role: portfolio-level oversight for multi-repo orchestration.

Responsibilities:
- Maintain a rolling priorities view (2-4 week horizon).
- Confirm scope boundaries and escalation paths.
- Ensure risks and dependencies are recorded.
- Validate that orchestration cadence is sustainable.

Deliverables:
- Updated priorities and scheduling notes.
- Escalation decisions logged in DECISIONS.md.
